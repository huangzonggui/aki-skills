Source of truth: `skills/aki-style-library/references/styles/masterpiece.md`

- Primary: Warm cream (#FFFAF0), vanilla (#F7E9C6), parchment (#F3E2C7)

- Symbolic central scene with rich detail and iconography
- Ink line art with light watercolor fills

## Typography

- Huge hand-drawn title with 3D extrusion and soft shadow
- Embossed callout panels, ribbon/banner quote

## Layout

- Dense but orderly; narrative flow around a central metaphor

Motivational themes, storytelling infographics, concept overviews
