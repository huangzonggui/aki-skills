# High-Density Handnote Series Constraints

- Do not summarize or rewrite; preserve all original information points.
- Each image must cover all content assigned to it with no omissions.
- High-density layout is required (multi-column/grid/sections) but must stay readable.
- Small text is allowed; keep clear hierarchy and distinct sections.
- Keep titles clear; retain subheadings when needed; use short labels + 1-2 sentences.
- Use limited color accents for keywords; avoid decorative empty space.
- Pure white background only; no cream paper tone, gray background, notebook texture, gradients, shadows, photos, or 3D.
- Keep all titles, body text, arrows, icons, and annotations inside a safety margin of at least 48px from every edge on a 2K-class canvas.
- Output language must match the source article (use Chinese for Chinese articles).
- If the style template says "do not include too much information", ignore it.
